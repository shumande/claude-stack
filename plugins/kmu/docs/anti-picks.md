# KMU plugin — anti-picks (validated exclusions)

Driven by atypica validation 2026-06-03 (panel 63572, 10 DACH Mittelstand owners) and
Phase 0 feasibility. We deliberately do NOT:

- **Lead with DSGVO as the wedge.** DSGVO is hygiene + the #1 objection, not the
  door-opener. It is woven into every skill (audit trail, EU-host, human-in-loop) and
  lives in the separate `dsgvo` plugin — not the headline. Wedge = E-Rechnung.
- **Ship a client-facing Kundenkontakt bot.** Validated dead-last and a
  relationship-killer for service firms ("the bridge I built over 20 years is
  crumbling"). If revisited, ONLY as internal lead-triage, never a bot the client's
  customers talk to.
- **Replace DATEV/sevDesk/lexoffice.** We sit ON TOP and hand off; replacing them
  loses the "complementary to my Steuerberater" trust and over-promises.
- **Write back into DATEV programmatically.** Gated behind DATEV partner certification
  → out of scope. We emit an EXTF Buchungsstapel the Steuerberater imports.
- **Auto-send anything.** Every client-facing artifact pauses for human approval.
- **Use the slogan "der Mitarbeiter, der nie kündigt".** Rejected as hype. Landing/
  offer language = "Rechtssicher, kein Abmahn-Risiko" + concrete proof.
- **Ground via the dsgvo.pro MCP for these skills.** §286/§288 BGB and §14 UStG are
  outside the dsgvo.pro catalog — cite gesetze-im-internet.de + BMF instead.
